Privacy Policy

Glyph Accord

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

Glyph Accord is a casual puzzle game. This policy explains what information the app handles, how local progress is saved, and how advertising and security services may operate when enabled.

Information We Collect Directly

Glyph Accord does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, precise location, photos, contacts, messages, or payment information.

The game saves gameplay information such as level progress, unlocked levels, music preference, sound-effect preference, and ad pacing counters locally on your device. This information is used to continue your game and remember your settings.

Offline Play and Local Storage

The core game can be played without creating an online profile. Local progress and settings remain on the device unless you delete the app, reset device data, or use operating-system features that back up and restore app data.

Advertising and App Tracking Transparency

The app may request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission before advertising SDKs are configured. Your ATT choice controls whether the advertising identifier may be used for tracking as defined by Apple. If you decline tracking permission, you can still play the game.

Advertising configuration is requested dynamically for the app bundle. Advertising SDKs are initialized only when valid runtime configuration is available. Depending on your ATT choice and SDK behavior, advertising partners may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers when permitted, coarse device information, ad impressions, ad interactions, diagnostics, and fraud-prevention signals to deliver ads, measure performance, limit abuse, and improve reliability.

Security and Device Identifier

The app initializes a security SDK using the app bundle identifier and the device Identifier for Vendor (IDFV). This helps protect service integrity, detect abuse, and support safe operation of advertising or related services.

Third-Party Services

The app may integrate Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, InMobi, and HSSecurity. These services may process information under their own privacy policies when they are enabled by valid configuration. The app does not use social login, online leaderboards, public player profiles, or cloud account features.

Children's Privacy

Glyph Accord is designed as an approachable puzzle game. We do not knowingly request personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service connected with the app, contact us so we can review the issue.

Your Choices

You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also delete local game progress by deleting the app from your device. Device-level privacy, backup, and advertising controls are managed by iOS.

Data Retention

Local progress and settings are kept on your device for as long as the app remains installed. Data processed by third-party SDKs is retained according to those providers' policies and operational requirements.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new update date.
