Privacy Policy

Glyph Accord

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

Glyph Accord is a casual puzzle game. The game stores progress and audio preferences locally on your device.

Advertising and Tracking

The app may request App Tracking Transparency permission before advertising SDKs are configured. Advertising configuration is requested dynamically for the app bundle. If tracking permission is declined, you can still play the game.

Advertising partners may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers when permitted, coarse device information, ad interaction data, and diagnostics to deliver ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

Security and Device Identifier

The app initializes a security SDK at launch using the app bundle identifier and the device vendor identifier to help protect service integrity and prevent abuse.

Data We Store

Game progress, unlocked levels, music preference, sound-effect preference, and ad pacing counters are stored locally. We do not require account registration.

Third Parties

The app may use Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, InMobi, and security services when enabled by valid runtime configuration.
